Next, i wanted to widen my scope of analysis to the entire wine dataset from all the 48 countries and see if there is any correlation between the wine price and ratings. From the chart below, you can see that there is no significant correlation between the price of varietals to its ratings. There seems to be very few high priced wines in the dataset and on the other hand there are many even higher rated wines in the under 50$ price range. This to us consumers is good news, since many higher rated varietals are available to us at an affordable price.
